The One with the Dash

An original endless-runner with a cozy, Friends-inspired vibe.

How to play

  • Swipe or use arrow keys to switch lanes (3 lanes).
  • Jump to avoid obstacles, slide to dodge low barriers.
  • Collect coffee cups for points and hearts for extra lives.
